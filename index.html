You take thousands of pictures each year on a plethora of devices. It is expected that sometimes you forget that you have already uploaded your pictures. What you end up with is hunderds of duplicate pictures taking up a lot of space and generally creating bothersome issues. Picasa Deduper will scan your Picasa library and show you which of your photos are duplicates. You can choose to delete the duplicate pictures and clear up valuable space in your library to hold more of your memories!

We store none your pictures or your information. We simply connect to your Picasa account, do our business, and show ourselves out. You can purchase any number of dedupes based on your needs. Your first dedupe is halfprice!
